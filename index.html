Turkish Inscriptions in Mongolia

View Turkish Inscriptions in Mongolia on GitHub

For thousands of years, Turkic people spread far and wide across Eurasia, with Mongolia of particular importance in the Study of Turkish history on account of the numerous ancient monuments which provide valuable evidence for archaeologists, linguists, and anthropologists. The monuments are concentrated in the Orhun, Tola, Ongin, and Selenge river basins.

These inscriptions on the monuments listed on this website are the earliest documents on the Turkish language and shed light on both the history and culture of their time.Some technological descriptions of this website is in the About section.
