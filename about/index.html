Homepage:
My homepage gives a brief introduction to Turkish inscriptions and the material displayed on this website. By creating a looping in the index as posts under categories, I manage to display my inscriptions and site descriptions under the categories of inscription names. The theme “jekyll-theme-time-machine” is really helpful in displaying a header on the top part of the webpage for the categories accordingly when you scroll down to each of them.

Posts: My contents 1 & 2 are shown in posts since I do want to update them over times and wish to display the date of my update.

    I use names of the main characters in the inscription as categories. Because of the different aims of mine towards pages and posts, these categories only apply to posts. The reason why I use character name as categories instead of languages or locations is that some of them cluster in the same region, and usually multilingually display texts of similar content.
